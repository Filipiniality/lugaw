by friends and fellow mangaka

By Tatsuki Fujimoto. This has a special place in my heart because it is literally the reason I started reading the manga. I love the detail of his silhouette not appearing through the curtains.

Credited to a longtime assistant. It was made during production, so this is possibly the first ever fanart? I think their toxic power couple energy really comes through here <3

Credited to a friend who has since deactivated their twitter account. Mio ended up being my fav character outside of the main couple, and I think the artist made her look super cute and dainty!

Credited to a friend with no web presence. Very cute and simple style that harkens back to the early chapters.

To be quite honest, I think there is an uncanniness to this drawing - and it turns out that was intentional, because this was made by Shuzo Oshimi aka the creator of Blood on the Tracks! Aono's face structure really reminds me of Seiko... anyway, I also recommend BotT. Nothing supernatural there, but has the same kind of dread.

Credited to Taku Kuwabara, the creator of Drifting Dragons. Ngl I actually said "woah" out loud the first time I saw this.

Credited to a friend of the author. I realize this is the only drawing of Fujimoto I have on this page. Kind of befitting, considering his role in Aono and Kariya's relationship.

Credited to Hiro Kiyohara. Sadly, this is the highest res I could find. At first glance, the lighting on Aono's hair makes it look like the short hairstyle he had in the earlier chapters :(
